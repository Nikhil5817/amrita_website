Accountancy Course

The Accountancy course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in financial reporting, analysis, and principles of accounting. The course covers a wide range of topics, from bookkeeping to advanced financial management.

Through the study of accountancy, students will develop skills to understand, interpret, and communicate financial information critical to business decision-making.

The course includes:

- Study of fundamental accounting concepts, including assets, liabilities, and equity.
- Exploration of financial statement preparation and analysis.
- Introduction to cost accounting and management accounting principles.
- Application of accounting standards and regulations.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Proficiency in recording financial transactions and preparing financial statements.
- Ability to analyze and interpret financial data for business evaluation.
- Understanding of budgeting, costing, and financial planning.
- Clear communication of financial insights to stakeholders.
