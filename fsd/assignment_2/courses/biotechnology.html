Biotechnology Course

The Biotechnology course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of biological processes and their applications in various sectors. The course covers a wide range of topics, from molecular biology to genetic engineering and bioinformatics.

Through the study of biotechnology, students will develop skills to manipulate and utilize biological systems for solving real-world challenges.

The course includes:

- Study of molecular biology techniques, including DNA manipulation and protein analysis.
- Exploration of genetic engineering methods and applications in medicine and agriculture.
- Introduction to bioprocessing and industrial applications of biotechnology.
- Application of bioinformatics tools for data analysis in biology.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Proficiency in laboratory techniques for genetic analysis and manipulation.
- Ability to apply biotechnological solutions to healthcare and environmental problems.
- Understanding of ethical considerations in biotechnology research.
- Application of bioinformatics tools to biological data interpretation.
