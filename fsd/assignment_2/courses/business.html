Business Administration Course

The Business Administration course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles, management strategies, and organizational dynamics. The course covers a wide range of topics, from marketing and finance to leadership and strategic planning.

Through the study of business administration, students will develop skills to navigate the complexities of the corporate world, make informed decisions, and lead teams effectively.

The course includes:

- Study of core business functions such as marketing, finance, and human resources.
- Exploration of management theories, organizational behavior, and leadership principles.
- Introduction to business ethics, corporate social responsibility, and sustainability.
- Application of strategic thinking and problem-solving in business contexts.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Proficiency in analyzing business environments and making strategic decisions.
- Ability to communicate effectively and collaborate in diverse teams.
- Understanding of financial analysis, budgeting, and investment strategies.
- Leadership skills to manage people and drive organizational growth.
