Economics Course

The Economics course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of economic principles, theories, and their real-world applications. The course covers a wide range of topics, including microeconomics, macroeconomics, and quantitative analysis.

The course includes:

- Study of microeconomic concepts such as supply and demand, consumer behavior, and market structures.
- Examination of macroeconomic factors including economic growth, inflation, and unemployment.
- Application of quantitative tools to analyze economic data and trends.
- Exploration of global economic issues and international trade.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Critical analysis of economic data and trends.
- Ability to formulate and evaluate economic policies.
- Effective communication of economic concepts and arguments.
- Problem-solving using economic reasoning and models.
