International Business Course

The International Business course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of global business operations, cross-border trade, and international market dynamics. The course covers a wide range of topics, from global economics to cultural considerations in international trade.

Through the study of international business, students will develop skills to navigate the challenges and opportunities of the global marketplace, analyze cross-cultural interactions, and make strategic decisions.

The course includes:

- Study of international trade theories and global economic systems.
- Exploration of multinational corporations, global supply chains, and international finance.
- Introduction to cultural diversity and its impact on business negotiations.
- Application of international marketing strategies and global business law.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Proficiency in analyzing global market trends and opportunities.
- Ability to formulate and execute international business strategies.
- Understanding of global financial management and risk assessment.
- Cross-cultural communication and negotiation skills.
