Physics Course

The Physics course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a deep understanding of fundamental principles of the physical world, ranging from classical mechanics to modern quantum theory. The course covers a wide range of topics, from motion to the behavior of particles at the atomic level.

Through the study of physics, students will develop a scientific mindset, analytical skills, and an appreciation for the laws that govern the universe.

The course includes:

- Exploration of classical mechanics, including motion, forces, and energy.
- Study of electromagnetism, including electric and magnetic fields.
- Introduction to modern physics concepts such as relativity and quantum mechanics.
- Application of physics principles to understand natural phenomena.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Ability to analyze and solve complex physical problems.
- Understanding of mathematical and experimental aspects of physics.
- Clear communication of scientific ideas and findings.
- Application of physics concepts in technology and engineering.
