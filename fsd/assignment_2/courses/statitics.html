Statistics Course

The Statistics course at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetam is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of statistical methods, data analysis, and their applications in various fields. The course covers a wide range of topics, from probability theory to advanced statistical modeling.

Through the study of statistics, students will develop skills to analyze data, make informed decisions, and contribute to research in diverse domains.

The course includes:

- Study of probability theory and its applications in real-world scenarios.
- Exploration of descriptive and inferential statistics techniques.
- Introduction to statistical software tools for data analysis.
- Application of statistical methods in research and decision-making.

Key skills that students will develop:

- Proficiency in data collection, organization, and analysis.
- Ability to interpret and communicate statistical results.
- Understanding of experimental design and hypothesis testing.
- Application of statistical methods to solve practical problems.
